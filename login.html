Note: For Google Sign-In to work properly, please ensure that third-party cookies are enabled in your browser settings. This is required because the login process involves cookies from Google domains.

How to enable third-party cookies:
  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data > Allow all cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Enhanced Tracking Protection > Custom > Uncheck "Cookies"
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data > Allow sites to save and read cookie data
  • Safari (macOS): Preferences > Privacy > Uncheck "Prevent cross-site tracking"
